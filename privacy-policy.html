Privacy Policy

Last updated:

At Rebelvids Academy ("we", "us", "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name and email when you sign up or log in.
  • Purchase and transaction details related to our courses, bundles, and digital products.
  • Usage data like pages visited and interactions to improve our services.
  • Device and technical data (browser, OS) collected via cookies and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and provide requested services.
  • To process orders, deliver digital content, and provide support.
  • To improve site performance, content quality, and user experience.
  • To communicate important updates, order confirmations, and policy changes.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and analyze usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may impact some features.

4. Payments

Payments are processed by trusted third-party providers. We do not store your full payment details on our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers who assist in operating our website and delivering services, under confidentiality obligations, or when required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided us information, contact us to remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
